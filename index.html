Congratulations! You are getting married!

What an exciting time in your life.

It would be a joy and an honor for me to help you create and experience a meaningful, memorable, and beautiful wedding day.

I can help you create personal, meaningful ceremonies that give expression to your beliefs about life, love, joy, and hope.

I will listen to what is important and meaningful to you. As we work together, I hope to help alleviate any stress you may be experiencing. With your input I will be able to customize a memorable and inspirational ceremony that celebrates you and your love for each other. I will direct and guide the service in a calm, smooth, joyous, and enthusiastic manner.

This can be expressed through all kinds of rituals, symbols, art, music readings that express your heritage, and cultural or religious beliefs. Traditional vows used by grandparents and parents can be used, or vows can be created by the couple. Families and children can be incorporated into the service as well.

I can help you make your wedding service uniquely yours.

  • Weddings Types:
  • Traditional
  • Same Gender
  • Religious
  • Interfaith
  • Non Religious
  • Multicultural
  • Renewal of Wedding Vows

Consultation rehearsal provided before wedding.

About Me

I have worked in human services in the fields of psychology and spirituality for many years.

I have Masters degrees in Education, theology and counseling. I've worked in schools, hospitals, colleges, religious institutions, and counseling and wellness centers. I've worked as teacher, counselor, chaplain, retreat director, trainer creator and director of programs.

My skills include: public speaking to large and small groups, creating and leading many types of religious and non-religious services, teaching mindfulness meditation and stress management, caring and serving others, especially at important life events.
