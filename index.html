Congratulations! You are getting married!

What an exciting time in your life.

It would be an honor for me to help you create and experience a meaningful, memorable, and beautiful wedding day.

I can help you create a personal, joyful ceremony that gives expression to your beliefs about life, love, and hope.

Service

Service Includes

Consultation

I will listen to what is important and meaningful to you. Email me to schedule an initial call, so I can learn more about who you are as a couple, and what kind of wedding you want. I can also guide you through the process of how to get a Connecticut marriage license.

Rehearsal

As we work together, I hope to help alleviate any stress you may be experiencing. With your input I will be able to customize a memorable and inspirational ceremony that celebrates you and your love for each other.

Ceremony

I will direct and guide the service in a calm, smooth, joyous, and enthusiastic manner. This can be expressed through all kinds of rituals, symbols, art, music, readings that express your heritage, and cultural or religious beliefs. Traditional vows used by grandparents and parents can be used, or vows can be created by the couple. Families and children can be incorporated into the service as well.

About

I have worked in human services in the fields of psychology and spirituality for many years.

I have Masters degrees in Education, theology and counseling.

I've worked in schools, hospitals, colleges, religious institutions, and counseling and wellness centers.

I've worked as a teacher, counselor, chaplain, retreat director, trainer, creator and director of programs.

I'm proud of my Irish heritage, and it's been an honor to perform weddings at home in the United States and in Ireland.

My skills include:

  • public speaking to large and small groups
  • creating and leading many types of religious and non-religious services
  • teaching mindfulness meditation and stress management
  • caring for and serving others, especially at important life events
